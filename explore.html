Tips


how to calm down

  • Think about someone or something that makes you happy
  • Listen to calming music
  • Doodle or draw
  • Create nice scenarios in your head
  • Find something that moves and focus on it
  • List five things you can touch, four things you can hear, three things you can taste, two things you can smell, and one thing you can see
  • Talk to someone you trust, if possible
  • breath in for 4 seconds
  • hold your breath for 7 seconds
  • exhale breath for 8 seconds

    • how to look like you weren't crying

  • Pat your cheeks, nose, forehead, and all around your neck with cold water
  • Hold a cold rag/tissue to your eyes and anywhere else that tends to get red or blotchy for two minutes
  • Regulate your breathing so your blood flow evens out

    • how to help someone having a panic attack

  • Stay calm
  • Don't yell
  • Ask if they need anything
  • Help them refocus their breathing
  • Say reassuring things in a soothing voice
  • Don't make it a big deal
