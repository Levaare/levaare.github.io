Tips

  • Think about someone or something that makes you happy
  • Listen to calming music
  • Doodle or draw
  • Create nice scenarios in your head
  • Find something that moves and focus on it
  • List five things you can touch, four things you can hear, three things you can taste, two things you can smell, and one thing you can see
  • Talk to someone you trust, if possible
